Our Rooms

At Hail Mary Nursing Home, we offer a variety of comfortable and well-equipped rooms to meet the needs of our residents. Each room is designed to provide a safe and welcoming environment.

Private Rooms

Our private rooms offer residents their own personal space, complete with a bed, dresser, nightstand, and a private bathroom. These rooms are ideal for those who value privacy and independence.

Shared Rooms

For residents who prefer companionship, we offer shared rooms that accommodate two individuals. Each resident has their own bed and storage space, while sharing a bathroom and common area.
