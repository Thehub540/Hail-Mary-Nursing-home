Our Services

At Hail Mary Nursing Home, we offer a wide range of services to ensure the well-being and comfort of our residents. Our dedicated team is committed to providing personalized care tailored to each individual's needs.

Medical Care

We provide 24/7 medical care with a team of experienced nurses and healthcare professionals. Our services include medication management, routine health assessments, and coordination with physicians.

Rehabilitation Services

Our rehabilitation services include physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy to help residents regain their independence and improve their quality of life.

Personal Care

We offer assistance with daily living activities such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and meal preparation to ensure our residents' comfort and dignity.

Social and Recreational Activities

We organize a variety of social and recreational activities to keep our residents engaged and active. These include arts and crafts, music therapy, exercise classes, and community outings.

Nutritional Services

Our nutritionists work closely with residents to create balanced and delicious meal plans that cater to individual dietary needs and preferences.
